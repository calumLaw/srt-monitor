Support

Safe SRT is a diagnostic monitor and player for SRT and HLS streams.

Getting started

  • SRT: choose a mode (Caller / Listener / Rendezvous), enter the host, port, and any options (latency, stream ID, encryption passphrase), then Connect. You can also paste a full srt://… URL or scan a QR code.
  • HLS: switch the protocol to HLS, paste an .m3u8 URL (or tap Try a demo stream), then Play.

What you can monitor

  • Live link metrics (RTT, bitrate, packet loss, buffer) at 4 Hz.
  • TR 101 290 transport-stream health (Priority 1 & 2) and per-PID bitrates.
  • Stream format (codec, resolution, frame rate), with optional video + audio.
  • 30 minutes of history with charts, threshold alerts, and JSON export.

Troubleshooting

  • It won't connect to my SRT source. Check the mode, host/port, and that any firewall/NAT allows the UDP port. For listener mode, the caller must be able to reach this device (see the in-app Documentation → Networking).
  • Does it work over the internet? Yes, if the path/port is reachable. Cellular networks often block inbound connections (CGNAT), so use Caller or Rendezvous.
