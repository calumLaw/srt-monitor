Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

SRT Monitor is a diagnostic monitor and player for SRT and HLS video streams.

SRT Monitor does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No accounts, no sign-in. The app has no user accounts.
  • No analytics or tracking. There are no analytics SDKs, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking of any kind.
  • No data leaves your device on our behalf. Network connections are made only to the stream endpoints (SRT/HLS URLs) that you enter. We operate no servers and receive no data from the app.
  • Your saved streams stay on your device. Connection details you save (host, port, options, and — only if you explicitly opt in — a passphrase) are stored locally on your device and never uploaded.
  • Camera. Camera access is used solely, on-device, to scan a QR code containing a stream's connection details. No images are captured, stored, or transmitted.
  • Local network. The app may request local-network access to reach stream sources on your network. This is used only to connect to the endpoints you specify.

Because SRT Monitor collects no data, there is nothing to request, correct, or delete from us.
